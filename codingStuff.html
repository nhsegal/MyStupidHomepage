About
I first started to explore coding by dabbling in Python many years ago. Solving problems on Project Euler taught me about recursion, data types, big-O notation. I later explored physical computing with Arduino microcontrollers to see if they could be incorporated into my physics classes. That led me to Processing and eventually to p5.js. I have explored vPython and Glowscript and incorporated some coding in these languages in my physics classes. I have also completed some online courses in data science and machine learning.

Most recently, I have worked through Harvard's CS50 online course. Upon completing that I have begun to work my way through The Odin Project curriculum in full stack web development. Below are my Odin Project submissions listed in reverse chronological order.
