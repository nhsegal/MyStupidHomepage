Welcome!
I am a physics teacher who has dabbled a little in coding over the years, and this website hosts some of the math- and physics-related simulations I've coded. Some of these simulations I have used in the classroom or for online teaching. Others are were pursued out of personal interest. Many of the simulations were developed using the fabulous p5.js library, which unfortunately means they won't run in the Safari browsers but should work in Firefox or Chrome. Also, I have not attempted to make the simulations compatible with tablets or mobile browsers. Nonetheless, I hope they may be of use to others.
